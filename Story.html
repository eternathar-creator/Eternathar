ORIGIN

Why ETERNATHAR exists?




"ETERNATHAR was born from a simple observation ; the Muslim world has thousands of apps for Quran and Athkar but almost nothing that prepares young muslims to lead and build their communities."
-THE FOUNDER
We looked around and saw platforms that were either purely religious or purely international. Almost nothing that spoke to a young Muslim who wants to learn about the challenges facing the Muslim world, develop new skills,and become active contributor to society.

So we built one. A free, youth-led platform where Muslim teens can explore meaningful topics, watch educational content, develop new skills, and contribute educational articles and videos.
