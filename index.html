Frequently Asked Questions

Is Eternathar really free? +
Yes! 100% free, always. No ads, no paywalls, no hidden fees. Eternathar is built for Muslim teens worldwid to access without barriers.
How do I become a volunteer? +
Fill out the volunteer application form, choose a track, and our team will reach out with next steps.
Do i get a certificate? +
Yes! Every volunteer who completes their track receives a free digital certificate.
What age group is this for? +
Eternathar was built specifically for Muslim teens worldwide; content and volunteer opoortunities are designed with that age group in mind.
